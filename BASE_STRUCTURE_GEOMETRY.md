The universe is modeled as a finite or countably infinite Tensor Network:
$$\mathcal{N} = (V, E, \sim)$$

- **E (Edges/Legs)**: Entanglement bonds or causal connections between tensors.
- **$\sim$ (Equivalence)**: Gauge invariance under local unitary transformations.
